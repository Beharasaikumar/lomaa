7. Which algorithm is commonly used for time series forecasting of stock prices?

8. What does the "stationarity" of a time series mean in the context of stock price forecasting?

Correct Option: B) The statistical properties of the series do not change over time

Solution and Explanation:
A stationary time series has constant mean, variance, and autocorrelation over time. Many time series forecasting models, like ARIMA, assume stationarity.

**Key Points of Stationarity**:
1. Mean, variance, and autocorrelation remain constant over time.
2. Stationary data is easier to model and forecast.

Correct Option: B) To make the time series stationary by removing trends

Solution and Explanation:
Differencing is used to subtract the current value of the series from the previous value to remove trends and achieve stationarity, which is often required for accurate forecasting.

**Differencing Process**:
1. Subtract current value from previous value.
2. Helps stabilize the mean of the series.

Correct Option: B) Elbow method
